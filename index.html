Challenges in the IL Market

Serious Lack of Market Information

  • IMS is not covering the IL market
  • Due to the reimbursement system prices are kept in strict confidentiality

This ends up in loss of revenues and opportunities

Unclear Regulatory Environment

  • Vague regulations
  • Frequent changes
  • Strong bureaucracy

This ends up in loss of filings and delays in launch time

HMOs Use Their Monopolistic Position

  • Highly conservative, close supply deals only with companies they have long-term relationship
  • Demand competitive supply price

This ends up in loss of revenue and market share
